Pluckt — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Pluckt ("the app", "we") is a guitar-theory learning app. This policy explains what data the app handles. Pluckt has no user accounts and does not ask you to sign in.

Data stored on your device

Your learning progress, placement results, practice streak, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to us and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Data collected through third-party services

Pluckt uses a small number of third-party services that process limited data on our behalf:

  • Crash and diagnostics (Sentry). If the app crashes or hits an error, technical diagnostic data is sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs and improve stability. This may include your device model, operating system version, app version, a non-identifying installation identifier, and the technical details of the error. It does not include your name, email, or any content you enter. See Sentry's privacy policy.
  • Purchases (Google Play Billing & RevenueCat). If you buy Pluckt Pro, your purchase is processed by Google Play and by RevenueCat, which manages and restores your purchase. The data involved includes purchase status, the product purchased, an anonymous app-user identifier, your country, and basic device information. We never see or store your payment card details — those are handled entirely by Google Play. See RevenueCat's privacy policy and Google's privacy policy.

Microphone (tuner)

The built-in tuner uses your device's microphone to detect the pitch of your guitar. Audio is analyzed live on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere. The microphone is only active while the tuner screen is open, and access is requested only when you first use the feature.

What we do not collect

Pluckt does not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or files. The app plays guitar sounds but The app does not record or store audio — the tuner's microphone input is analyzed live and immediately discarded.. We do not use advertising, advertising identifiers, or behavioral/usage tracking, and we do not sell your data.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily practice reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us or to any server.

How data is protected and shared

Data sent to the services above is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. These providers act as our processors and use the data only to provide their service (crash reporting and purchase management). We do not share your data with other parties.

Data retention and deletion

The crash and purchase data described above is anonymous and not tied to a personal account. To request deletion of any data associated with your device or purchase, contact us at the email below and we will action it with the relevant provider.

Children

Pluckt is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
