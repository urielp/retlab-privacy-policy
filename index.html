Privacy Policy

App: FretLab
Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

FretLab is a guitar theory learning app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

FretLab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your learning progress is stored locally on your device only.

Permissions

FretLab requests access to audio playback to play guitar sounds. The app does not record audio or access your microphone.

Third-Party Services

FretLab does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

FretLab does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
